Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Each college is allowed to field a maximum of two teams in the competition.
  • The use of any form of hacking, cheating, or third-party software providing an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.
  • Teams must arrive on time for matches; tardiness may result in disqualification.
  • Each participating team must consist of a fixed number of four members.
  • Violation of the rules may lead to disqualification
  • The decision of the judges and event organisers is final.
    • There will be Three Rounds:-
    • First & Second Round in an Elimination Round
    • Final Round: TDM Match
    • First Round
    • This round of the BGMI Collegiate Clash will be exclusively played on the Erangel map.
    • The winning team of this Erangel round will secure direct qualification to the final round of the BGMI Collegiate Clash.
    • All teams, except the Erangel round-winning team, will proceed to the second round of the BGMI Collegiate Clash.
    • Second Round
    • This round of the BGMI Collegiate Clash also played on the Erangel map.
    • As per First Round the winning team of this Erangel round will secure direct qualification to the final round of the BGMI Collegiate Clash.
    • Final Round: TDM Match
    • The Final round of the BGMI Collegiate Clash will be a Team Deathmatch (TDM) round.
    • The TDM Final Round will exclusively feature Sniper Rifles. All other guns are strictly prohibited, including pistols.
    • The team emerging victorious in the Sniper Showdown TDM Final Round will be crowned the overall winner of the BGMI Collegiate Clash.
