Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Two participants will be allowed from each college.
  • Theme:- Nature Participants will have to follow the theme for the competition.
  • Use of any color medium would be allowed.
  • The time limit will be 1 hour to make the poster.
  • The participants will only be given a white paper sheet to make the poster, rest materials will have to be taken by the participant on their own.
  • Judgment criteria: Creativity, Usage of colours, and theme's impact.
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances./li>
  • The decision of the judges and event organisers is final.
